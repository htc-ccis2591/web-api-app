How high's the water, Mama?

Spring in Minnesota and, as usual, it is still cold! We need a break so how about if we run down to sunny Florida for a few days and do some sailing?

Before we head out it's probably a good idea to check on the tides. We don't want to set sail if the water is too low and risk breaking the keel off the boat. Nobody likes to sink. And we don't want to find ourselves fighting against the tide on our way in or out. That's makes for lots of hard work on the boat while making very little progress. Better to go with tide than against it.
